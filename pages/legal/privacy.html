◆ Your privacy and data security are important to us ◆

1. Introduction

Zeptro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Zeptro website and gaming platform.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information from you in various ways, including:

  • Account Registration: Username, email address, password, date of birth, nationality, and identification information
  • Profile Information: Gaming statistics, achievements, preferences, and gameplay history
  • Communication: Messages, support tickets, and feedback you submit to us
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method (processed securely by third-party providers)
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit or use Zeptro, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Game performance metrics and in-game behavior
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Analytics data and usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your gaming account
  • To provide and improve our gaming services
  • To process transactions and send related information
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity and abuse
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • To send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To respond to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • To analyze user behavior and improve our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting business, or serving you
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Public Information: Your username and gaming achievements may be visible to other players
  • Your Consent: We may share information with your explicit permission

5. Data Security

Zeptro implements comprehensive technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password hashing and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular employee training on data protection

Note: No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Zeptro uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Some features of the Service may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
